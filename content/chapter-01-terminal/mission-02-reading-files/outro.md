# Comfortable with files

**Sage** sips coffee. "Reading log files is 80% of the job. The other 20%
is changing them - which is what we're doing next."

> Quick mental model:
>
> - **`cat`** is for *short* files you want all at once.
> - **`head` / `tail`** for *peeking*.
> - **`less`** for *exploring*.
>
> You'll mix all three constantly. They become muscle memory pretty fast.

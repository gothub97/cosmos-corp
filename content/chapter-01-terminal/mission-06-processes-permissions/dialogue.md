# Processes & permissions

**Sage** flags you down.

> "Two more things you need to feel comfortable with before we leave the
> terminal chapter:
>
> 1. **Processes** - the things actually *running* on the machine. `ps`
>    shows them; `kill` (or `pkill`) ends them.
> 2. **Permissions** - what each user is allowed to *do* with a file. The
>    one you'll touch most often is `+x`: making a script executable.
>
> I left a runaway `sleep` background process going. Find it, kill it, then
> make the deploy script executable."
